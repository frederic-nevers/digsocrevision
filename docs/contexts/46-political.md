|----------|------------------|------------------|------------------|
| üó≥Ô∏è Voting and campaigning | - Online voter registration <br/> - Mobile voting apps ([Voatz](https://voatz.com/)) <br/> - Digital canvassing tools ([NGP VAN](https://www.ngpvan.com/), [Ecanvasser](https://www.ecanvasser.com/), [NationBuilder](https://www.nationbuilder.com/)) | - Increased voter turnout and engagement <br/> - Improved accessibility for remote or disabled voters | - Concerns about security and integrity of digital voting systems <br/> - Potential for voter suppression or intimidation |
